#WIZ750SR-100-EVB

  • WIZ750SR-100 Developer Board.
  • USB to UART chip, CP2104.
  • RESET Tact SW.
  • BOOT0 Slide SW.
  • H/W Trig Slide SW.
  • Expansion GPIO TEST (Digital / Analog)
  • Micro USB.
  • WIZ750SR-100-EVB (RS-232)
    • RS-232C Transceiver, D-SUB9-MALE.
