## WIZ750SR-EVB

  - WIZ750SR Developer Board.
  - USB to UART chip, CP2104.
  - RESET Tact SW.
  - BOOT0 Slide SW.
  - H/W Trig Slide SW.
  - Expansion GPIO TEST (Digital / Analog)
  - Micro USB.
  - WIZ750SR-EVB (TTL/232)
      - RS-232C Connector, D-SUB9-MALE.
  - WIZ750SR-EVB (422/485)
      - RS-422/485 Connector, ECH381R.
